Areas: Level 3

Below is the key for level 3. All the images provided by the official SU website.

Below is the map from the SU which shows the entire plan of level 3, which is the main ground floor which has the main reception desk, SSID, many places to eat and to socialise or even grab a tea/coffee.

SSiD and Student Advice Centre

The SSiD is the "one-stop shop for student-related enquires and administrative services", and will the first place to visit/contact for any issue to do with the university during your time here.

The SSiD has been voted as the number 1 student service in the UK according to International and UK students for the last 10 years, and if they can't help directly they will be able to refer you to the correct location for any university issues.

The desk is located near the south entrance of the SU building. For more information visit the official SU's web page on the SSiD.

Our Shop

'Our shop' is the SU's "very own local supermarket" located on the main floor level 3 in the SU building. It's the best place to pick up any snacks, drinks or stationery you might need in a pinch.

They offer freshly baked breakfasts, lunch-time meal deals and a 3-for-2 stationary offers that runs all year round. All money the shop makes goes straight back into the SU which helps in every aspect of the SU and the SU building.

For more information visit the official SU's web page on 'Our Shop'.

Coffee Revolution

Coffee Revolution is the SU's own coffee shop and is a great place to "meet friends, study, or just to take time out to treat yourself". They offer a variety of different coffees, teas, smoothies, as well as some breakfast options.

To find out more visit the official SU's web page on coffee revolution.

Food places

There are many places on the SU 3rd floor that offer different foods. You could grab a pasty at 'Proper Pasty' or a burrito at 'Grill & Go' or even a custom salad at 'new leaf'.

We talk about these various options in detail at our Foods page on our website. Or you can visit these respective links on the SU's web page for menus and more info:

  • Proper Pasty
  • Grill & Go
  • New Leaf

Other

There are many other things to do on level 3 of the SU, such as using the activities and sports zone to go to the respective sports offices, or even meet and play table tennis and sit to have a drink there.

Level 3 also is where the welcome desk can be found, as well as the box office and many other services all shown on the map above.

To find out more visit the SU's website.
