Areas: Level 2

Below is the key for level 2. All the images provided by the official SU website.

Below is the map from the SU which shows the entire plan of level 2, complete with the 'Foundry', the SU Auditorium and many more.

Interval

One of the main attractions of the SU's level 2 is the 'Interval' cafe and bar which describes itself as a "laid back and sophisticated cafe bar serving a selection of fine wine, continental beers, Fairtrade hot drinks and tasty cuisine".

We describe more about the Interval in our Food page. And you can view the menu and more information on the Interval cafe and bar on the Official SU's web page on the Interval.

Foundry

The foundry is the SU's night club which hosts a variety of different types of events each night such as 'ROAR', 'Pop Tarts' and special themed nights. All of this along with cheap and affordable drinks makes the foundry a must try venue for students.

More information on the foundry can be found on the Official Foundry web page.

Auditorium

The auditorium or Nelson Mandela Auditorium is a lecture theatre and 'Film Unit' cinema, which hosts a variety of films for the public to book and buy tickets to old and new films which the 'Film Unit' has selected.

Both small indie films and big blockbusters are shown here, giving film lovers and people looking for a casual night out the chance to find something for them at this unique cinema.

To find out about the films being shown and to buy tickets visit the SU's page for the events.

Other

There are also some other things to do on level 2 that might be useful to know that it's there, such as, a prayer room, SU cash office, LGBT lounge, music room and other offices as shown on the map above.

The toilets next to the auditorium have also changed to gender-neutral toilets, and some of the offices are closed off at this time due to works being done on level 2.

To find out more visit the SU's website.
