Areas: Level 5

Below is the key for level 5. All the images provided by the official SU website.

Below is the map from the SU which shows the entire plan of level 5, which comprises mostly of conference rooms and the SU's 'Inox Dine'.

Inox Dine

On the 5th floor of the SU lies the high-end restaurant, cafe, and lounge bar 'Inox' which hosts business lunches, stylish evening dinners, afternoon tea and much more. It's currently being nominated for the 'Best high end restaurant in Sheffield' award in the 'Sheffield Social Awards'.

You can book a table here or visit their website to find out more about thier menu's, events and reviews for the restaurant.

Conference Rooms

The only other parts of the SU building level 5 are the conference rooms, which won't be available to book, but might be useful to know about if you're ever in need of going to the various conference rooms there.

To find out more visit the SU's website.
