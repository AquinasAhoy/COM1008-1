Areas: Level 1

Below is the key for level 1. All the images provided by the official SU website.

Inside level one of the SU, we have 'Bar One', a Students' Union bar which prides itself on its "burgers, quizzes, sport and friendly staff!". Bar one is the only part of the SU on level 1.

Bar One

Below is the map from the SU which shows the entire plan of 'Bar One', complete with a pool room, bar, toilets and even its hireable 'Raynor Lounge' for large parties.

You can visit our page on Food to find out more about bar one. Or you can view the menu's, book a space and find out more at the Official SU's webpage on 'Bar One'.
