Below is the key for level 6. All the images provided by the official SU website.

Below is the map from the SU which shows the entire plan of level 6, where you can find the exams and income office as well as student support guidance and others.

Offices

Level 6 in the SU building has various offices you might need to visit with various issues.

Firstly, is the University income office which is "responsible for providing information to help students with their financial enquiry". So if you ever need to visit the income office for financial help, it's located on level 6.

The Exam office is located right next to the income office and will help with any issues related to examinations.

The registry office is located right next to the exam office and deals with the "annual registration of current students".

Finally is the student support guidance office which is located at the end of level 6, and aims to help with any students issues with support whether that be personal or professional support needed for anyone at the University.

To find out more visit the SU's website.
