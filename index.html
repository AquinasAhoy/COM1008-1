Welcome

This site is designed to give new students information about the University of Sheffield Students' Union Building from the perspective of a current Computer Science student.

Here you can find out about what stores and services are available on each level as well as all the different eating options in the building. You can also get an idea of how you might use the area by looking at the typical week section to see how we use the SU building throughout our week.

If you have any issues with the site or comments of any kind, feel free to contact us.
