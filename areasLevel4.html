Areas: Level 4

Below is the key for level 4. All the images provided by the offical SU website.

Below is the map from the SU which shows the entire plan of level 4, complete with many different locations to do some studying and different places to eat and socialise.

Uni Central

Uni Central is the SU's upper floor food station which includes 'Snack it burger' and 'Dough pizza', places to get burgers and pizzas respectivley. If you feel like grabbing a burger or pizza this is the place to go.

We write more about the two restaurants in our Foods web page, or you can find out more infomation at the SU's web page about uni central.

The View Deli

The view deli is the SU's deli which hosts "freshly made vegetarian, vegan and gluten free treats and tasty lunches" on the 4th floor of the SU building.

We write more about the view deli in our Foods web page, or you can find out more infomation at the SU's web page about the view deli.

The Gallery

The Gallery is a students work area ideal for using the computers there, printing, or organising group meetings for projects. The gallery can also be a good place to get some work done in a small free or be a nice place to sit and eat if other places are full.

Other

Level 4 has other rooms and things to do as shown on the map above, such as; all the SU officer's offices, as well as Gellery meeting rooms and room bookings which can be made here.

To find out more visit the SU's website.
