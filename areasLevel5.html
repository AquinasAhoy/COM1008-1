Inox Dine

On the 5th floor of the SU lies the high end restaurant, cafe, and lounge bar 'Inox' which hosts business lunches, stylish evening dinners, afternoon tea and much more. It's currently being nominated for the 'Best high end resaurant in Sheffield' award in the 'Sheffield Social Awards'.

You can book a table here or visit their website to find out more about thier menu's, events and reviews for the resaurant.

Conference Rooms

The only other parts of the SU building level 5 are the conference rooms, which won't be avaliable to book, but might be useful to know about if you're ever in need of going to the various conference rooms there.

To find out more visit the SU's website.
