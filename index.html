Welcome

Hello and welcome to the "Students' Union" site, a student's guide to the Students' Union building at the University of Sheffield.

This site is designed to give new students information about the University of Sheffield Students' Union Building from the perspective of current Computer Science students. This site was built by 2 first-year students; Tom Tonner, and Joseph Barker who have made this website their mission to help any newcomers to the University make full use of the Students' Union building - especially those who are computer science students.

On the website, you can find various pages about different parts of the SU building, and their uses. These pages include:

  • The Areas page, which shows each floor of the SU and what you will find there.
  • The Typical Week page, which showcases what a typical week might be like for a computer science student using the SU building.
  • The Food page, which highlights all the places around the SU to get some food and we tell you our feelings and reviews about each.
  • The Contact page, which is used to contact either one of us about any issues.
  • The Accessibility page, which shows you our effort to make this website as user-friendly as possible
  • The Copyright page which provides our full permissions and use of copyrighted material.

If you have any issues with the site or comments of any kind, feel free to contact us and we hope you enjoy and find the website useful.
