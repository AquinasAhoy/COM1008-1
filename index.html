Welcome

Hello and welome to the "Students' Union" site, a students guide to the Students' Union building at the University of Sheffield.

This site is designed to give new students information about the University of Sheffield Students' Union Building from the perspective of current Computer Science students. This site was built by 2 first year students; Tom Tonner, and Joseph Barker who have made this website their mission to help any new comers to the University make full use of the Students' Union building - especially those who are computer science students.

On the website you can find various pages about different parts of the SU building, and their uses. These pages include:
